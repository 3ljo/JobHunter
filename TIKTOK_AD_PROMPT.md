## THE PROMPT (copy everything between the lines)

---

Create a **vertical 9:16 TikTok ad, 22 seconds total, 30 fps, cinematic
4K, ultra-sharp text, native phone-shot feel mixed with clean UI
overlays**. Use the reference images I uploaded as the on-screen
product shots — match colors, fonts, and the deep-purple/midnight-blue
palette (#0a0d24 background, #764DF0 / oklch(0.59 0.245 291) accent
purple). Brand name: **CvClimber**. Logo appears in shots 1, 5, and 7.

Energy: fast, punchy, Gen-Z TikTok pacing. Hard cuts every 1.5–3
seconds. Subtle whoosh + UI tap SFX on every cut. Background track:
upbeat lo-fi trap / hyperpop instrumental at ~120 BPM, ducked
under the voiceover. Voiceover is a confident young female (mid-20s,
American), conversational, slightly hyped — NOT corporate.

Captions: bold sans-serif (Inter Black or similar), white text with
thin black stroke, animated word-by-word in sync with the voiceover,
positioned in the center-lower third (above TikTok's UI). Every claim
on screen is also said out loud.

### GLOBAL DIRECTION NOTES

- Aspect ratio: **9:16 (1080×1920)**.
- Keep all critical text and logos inside the safe zone (avoid the
  bottom 250 px and the right 180 px where TikTok overlays the
  username and buttons).
- Color grade: cool teal shadows, warm magenta highlights, slight
  film grain, subtle vignette.
- Motion: every cut has a 4-frame motion blur or whip-pan transition
  to feel native to TikTok.
- Subtitles always on (TikTok plays muted by default).
- End frame must hold the logo + URL for at least 1.2 s so the user
  can read it.
- Do NOT show fake competitor logos. Only mention Google / Meta /
  Stripe as employer names with subtle text chips, no logos.
- Do NOT show real personal data on the CV — blur or use placeholder
  names.
