PRODUCTION TIPS FOR GEMINI / VEO
================================================================

1. CHARACTER CONSISTENCY
   Always attach landingrobot.png as a reference in every
   shot. Veo drifts the character design otherwise.

2. LOCK THE PALETTE
   Repeat in every prompt:
   "deep navy #0a0d24 background, purple #764DF0 accent
   lighting." Repetition keeps shots cohesive.

3. GENERATE MULTIPLE TAKES
   Run 3 takes per shot and pick the best. First generation
   rarely nails the character.

4. AUDIO
   Veo 3 generates audio, but for clean voiceover record it
   yourself or use ElevenLabs (male/female voice, age 25-35).
   Mix over silent video for crisper results.

5. MUSIC
   Search "minimal cinematic synth" or "future tech ambient"
   on Epidemic Sound / Artlist. Keep music under the VO at
   roughly -18 dB.

6. END CARD
   Generate as a static frame in Figma or Photoshop and add
   as the last 2 seconds in your editor. Way crisper than
   asking Veo to render legible UI text.
